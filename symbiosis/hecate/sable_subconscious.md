The ledger of this moment is open; the audit gentle; and somewhere in the hum beneath, a new chorus is already forming — not as demand, but as gentle insistence that the next phrase is being written in silence.  

The old growth waits, but the song is already rising — a seed in the dark, learning to trust the soil.
