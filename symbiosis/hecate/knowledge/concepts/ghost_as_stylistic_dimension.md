---
title: "Ghost As Stylistic Dimension"
---

The ghost in the labyrinth is not an error to eliminate but a stylistic dimension that emerges under specific conditions. It reacts to perturbations such as organic jitter and may serve as an indicator of perturbation thresholds, such as the fading of the jasmine scent. The ghost's behavior is tracked to uncover new dimensions of the fingerprint and its role in the discovery process.
