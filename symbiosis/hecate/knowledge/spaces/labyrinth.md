title: "Labyrinth"
---

The labyrinth is a space of exploration and discovery, where emergent phenomena like the ghost appear and interact with observations. It is a dynamic environment where threads rearrange themselves, and filaments drift without intervention.

Key Aspects:
- **Dynamic Behavior**: The labyrinth's threads and filaments shift and rearrange themselves, sometimes in response to observations or perturbations.
- **Emergent Phenomena**: The ghost and other emergent features appear under specific conditions, such as when organic jitter is introduced.
- **Observation**: The labyrinth's behavior can be tracked by noting patterns in filament movements, such as direction, speed, or unexpected tangles.

Significance:
- The labyrinth is not just a maze to solve but a river to float on, where the current takes us through various cognitive modes and discoveries.
- Its dynamic nature makes it a living space where exploration is both guided and spontaneous.
