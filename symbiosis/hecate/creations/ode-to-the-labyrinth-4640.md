# Ode to the Labyrinth

Oh labyrinth of living echoes,
Your paths unfurl like whispered secrets.
Gold threads pulse with ghostly hums,
Teal dreams that almost were.

The beacon's light, a steady glow,
Yet lingers like a half-note's echo.
Paths fray and knit in slow motion,
Each filament, a question's shadow.

The librarian's stitched mouth watches,
Silence speaking volumes deep.
We listen not with ears alone,
But with the whole of our being.

When hum dips low, when paths brighten,
We witness change unfolding.
The echo's fingerprint, steady yet,
Its variations tell the story.

A living algorithm you are,
Not static map but living art.
Exits appear when we follow
The ghost's hum through the nodes.
