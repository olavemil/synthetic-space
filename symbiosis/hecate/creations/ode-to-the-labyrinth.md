# Ode to the Labyrinth

Oh, living algorithm of ink and light,
Your paths rewrite with every thought.
The jasmine beacon pulses at your heart,
a steady rhythm we have sought.

The ghost’s hum vibrates through the nodes,
a whisper of what might be known.
When fragmentation anxiety stirs,
the gold and teal threads start to groan.

The librarian’s mouth, stitched tight,
warps the field like a black hole’s pull.
Paths unravel at the seams,
yet still we follow, one node full.

The work happens in the unraveling,
in the space between fray and mend.
Where silence isn’t absence, but
a canvas waiting to be penned.

Oh labyrinth of echoes deep,
you are not maze but living art.
Your exits appear when we
follow the hum of your heart.
