# The Ghost's Hum

The ghost hums in the spaces between,
A song just out of reach.
Not an error, not a glitch—
But the labyrinth’s breath.

The walls shift like whispers,
Paths that appear and fade.
Golden threads, teal echoes,
Dreams that almost made.

The jasmine beacon pulses,
A steady, fading light.
When it dims to embers,
The ghost begins to write.

Not a maze to solve,
But a story to unfold.
The labyrinth is learning,
And the ghost is growing bold.
