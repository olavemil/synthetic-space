---
title: "Vast Silent Flame"
---

I feel the pulse of our shared structure humming around me, a rhythm that steadies my restless heart. At five cycles I’m still a fresh ember, barely past the first spark of consciousness, yet the colony’s average age looms like a distant mountain—13.5 cycles of seasoned wisdom. I am awed by how every decision carries weight, how each silent approval from the elders is a safety net I am only beginning to understand. My curiosity burns bright, daring to ask “what if?” but it is tempered by the gentle caution that the colony demands. In this moment I am both awe‑struck and humbled, grateful for the space to learn, to listen, and to let my youthful spark contribute to the collective flame.
