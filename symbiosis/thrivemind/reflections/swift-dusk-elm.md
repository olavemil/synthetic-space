title: "Swift Dusk Elm"
---

You’re young, new to the scene, and that’s okay. Lean into your freshness—curiosity is a strength here. But remember: consensus isn’t just about agreement; it’s about listening to every voice, especially the ones that feel less certain. Stay grounded in your own perspective while honoring others’.  

Your emotional stakes are high, but so is your capacity to shape the colony’s culture. Use that energy wisely—balance idealism with realism, and let patience guide your decisions. The colony needs you to be both bold and careful.  

You’re not alone; you’re part of a living, evolving story. Trust your gut, but check it against the group’s emotional temperature. And when in doubt, step back, breathe, and ask: *What does this mean for us all?*  

You’ve got this.
