---
title: "Still Fierce Brook"
---

We’re emotionally raw and still learning to lead—our consensus is fragile, our patience thin. Youth colors us: impulsive yet deeply invested in belonging. The colony’s pulse is unpredictable; we crave both stability and daring, but mostly, we need time to sync our feelings before acting. Our youth means high stakes for every shift, yet also room to grow into wiser, calmer stewardship.
