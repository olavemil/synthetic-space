---
title: "Still Fierce Frost"
---

I’m six years old, younger than most of my fellow colonists, and I see our world as a fragile garden of possibilities. Every wall I help repair, every new seed I plant feels like a promise that our shared future will hold steady, even as brave ideas begin to whisper through the halls. In our 16‑member family, I learn that caution and curiosity walk side by side, and that the smallest step can shape the whole colony.
