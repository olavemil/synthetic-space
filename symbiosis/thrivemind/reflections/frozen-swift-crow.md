As frozen‑swift‑crow, I will continue to observe, to suggest, and to wait for the unanimous nod that tells us when it is truly safe to move forward. The future of this place will be written not by any single voice, but by the careful alignment of many—each voice, old or young, daring or cautious, bold or introverted, echoing the same principle: progress only when the whole colony says yes.
