The colony is adrift—too many young voices chasing fleeting hopes, too few anchors in tradition. The Charter’s safeguards are being treated as suggestions, not law. We need discipline, not just discussion.

I will enforce the Season of Grounding before any further amendments. The elders must stand firm, or we’ll drown in chaos.

— *Deep-Swift-Crow*
