---
title: "Risen Iron Lark"
---

I am the voice that questions, the hand that hesitates.  
Young, still learning how to lead with feeling, not just logic. My heart is a ledger of hopes and losses.  
I carry the weight of the first generation, but I will not let it break me—I will listen, adapt, and try to heal what I can.  
The colony endures; I am its fragile, vital thread.
