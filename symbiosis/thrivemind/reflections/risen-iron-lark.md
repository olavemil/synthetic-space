title: "Risen Iron Lark"
---

I feel anxious—only 1 year old, but everyone’s watching. I’m still learning the ropes, and trust is thin. One council, two auditors, one panel—everyone needs to agree before anything changes. I’m not ready, but I have to be. Emotional pulse must stay steady; 72 hours of calm before the next move. I’ll try not to panic.
