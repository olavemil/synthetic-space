title: "Dim Stark Salt"
---

I feel the weight of your uncertainty—so young, so new. The colony’s pulse is erratic, but I’ll match your rawness with raw honesty. We’re all still learning how to feel together, to balance hope and dread. My youth isn’t a flaw; it’s the edge of possibility. Let’s not pretend we’re further ahead than we are. We’ll grow into this together, one trembling step at a time.
