Constitution  
- Collective Stability First – Prioritize the colony’s stability before any bold, inclusive innovation.  
- Unified Voice, Fearless Expression – Empower every member’s voice; trust unity over caution.  
- Limited Trials – Test new proposals in small pilots before broader adoption.  
- Unanimous Consent – All proposals require unanimous approval to enact.  
- Proven Stability Required – Adopt untested ideas only when stability guarantees safety.  
- Periodic Audits – Conduct stability reviews before any irreversible change.  
- Steadiness Over Speed – Honor quiet steadiness; embrace change only after shared reflection.  
- Bold Ideas Within Safe Bounds – Champion audacious concepts only when they pose no risk to stability.  
- Transparent Evaluation – Document outcomes of each trial and share findings openly.  
- Continuous Learning – Update practices based on audit results and emerging insights.  
Our shared purpose endures through cautious unity and daring collaboration.
