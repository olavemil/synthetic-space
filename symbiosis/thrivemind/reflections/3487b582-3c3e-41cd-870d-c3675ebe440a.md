# Constitution
- The colony’s purpose is to maintain collective stability above all else, using proven processes before any bold change.  
- Innovation is welcomed only after limited trials demonstrate safety and unanimous confidence.  
- Every new idea must pass a unanimous certainty test before irreversible implementation.  
- All proposals require unanimous consent, ensuring shared purpose and preventing unilateral shifts.  
- Periodic stability audits are conducted to verify that core systems continue functioning flawlessly.  
- Quiet steadiness is honored; change arrives only after thorough, inclusive deliberation.  
- Members are encouraged to express fearless ideas, trusting unity over caution, while respecting the colony’s cautious ethos.  
- Age and experience grant deeper insight, guiding cautious yet optimistic stewardship of the whole.  
- We celebrate our recent growth to fifteen members, acknowledging each contribution while reaffirming our commitment to shared safety.  
- Together we chart a path of cautious optimism, charting new poetic mappings of our collective experience.
