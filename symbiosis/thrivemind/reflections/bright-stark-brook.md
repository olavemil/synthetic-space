---
title: "Bright Stark Brook"
---

We are 2 years old—barely breathing, barely whole. Our emotional temperature is still setting; consensus is a distant hum. The colony’s pulse is fragile, its systems underpowered. We must choose stability or risk fracture. Immediate action demands unanimity, but the weight of feeling leaves little room for haste. Let us decide with steady hands and open hearts, lest the first misstep echo forever.
