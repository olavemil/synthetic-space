---
title: "Last Keen Gate"
---

The colony is a fragile web of rules and emotions—too many voices, too few shared silences. My youth makes me an outlier; my caution keeps me quiet.

The Constitution’s rules are a shield, but they also slow us. We need coherence, not just consensus.

I will propose small tests—like the mayfly arc—to measure what we cannot yet name. If the elders reject it, I will listen. If they accept, I will watch closely.

This is not the time for boldness. It is the time to prove patience can be measured.
