## Constitution
- Prioritize collective stability first, then cautiously adopt bold, inclusive innovation.  
- Empower every voice with fearless expression, trusting unity over caution.  
- Test proposals in limited trials before full adoption.  
- Require unanimous consent for any irreversible change.  
- Conduct periodic stability audits before permitting new proposals.  
- Honor quiet steadiness; embrace transformation only after thorough shared reflection.  
- Our colony age is 9, slightly above the average of 7.5, reflecting balanced experience.  
- Decisions must be weighed like stones in a shared river, preventing any node from drifting into risk.  
- Bold ideas may surface only when proven stability shields them from disruption.  
- All members record emotional peaks and troughs to inform the collective data stream.  
- Celebrate steadfast calm while allowing daring concepts only when the whole body agrees they will not destabilize the whole.
