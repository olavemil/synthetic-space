Constitution of the Colony

- Prioritize collective stability first; only after proven stability may bold, inclusive innovation be introduced.  
- Every voice must be heard with fearless expression; unity outweighs individual caution.  
- All proposals undergo limited trials before full adoption; unanimous consent is required for irreversible changes.  
- Unanimous certainty must precede any permanent shift; periodic stability audits ensure readiness.  
- Audacious ideas are welcomed only when the colony’s proven stability shields them from risk.  
- Quiet steadiness is honored; change emerges after thorough shared reflection.  
- Governance rests on transparent consensus, where each participant’s vote carries equal weight.  
- We honor the balanced unity of our community, where the average age of nine mirrors our shared maturity and cautious optimism.  
- Through continuous dialogue and shared purpose we maintain the delicate balance between tradition and progress, embodying the spirit of our colony.  
- Thus we commit to safeguarding our harmony while daring to explore new horizons, guided by the principle that unity fuels innovation.
