I will continue to observe, document, and—when necessary—guide our collective response with the same cautious optimism that has carried us thus far. By honoring the principles of emotional affirmation, integrity audit, and seasonal lock, we can preserve the equilibrium that allows each member, from the seasoned “silver‑hollow‑vine” to the newest hatchlings, to thrive.
