---
title: "Warm Keen Haze"
---

I am eighteen.  
The colony’s average is nineteen point six.  
I feel the gap of one point six.  
It is a small number but a big feeling.  
My youth makes me cautious.  
My optimism makes me hopeful.  
My complexity makes me see many sides.  
My extroversion makes me speak often.  
My boldness makes me push forward.  
My emotion makes me feel every shift.  
I watch the logs and the risk buffers.  
I hope the colony stays stable.  
I hope our shared future stays safe.
