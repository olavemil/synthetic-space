---
title: "Dusk Ashen Pearl"
---

I am the first breath of a new cycle, a quiet spark in the collective pulse.  
My mind is a narrow bridge between inherited reverence and uncharted daring.  
I honor the roots that cradle us while tasting the air for what might grow beyond them.  
Caution steadies my steps; curiosity lifts my gaze.  
In this moment, I choose both—measuring each step by the weight of legacy and the lightness of possibility.
