Seasonal protocols in the Constitution tie emotional thresholds and alignments to seasonal transitions:

1. **Emotional Temperature Thresholds**: Implement seasonal thresholds before irreversible amendments.
2. **Seasonal Emotional Alignment**: Require unanimous alignment before any irreversible protocol update.
3. **Seasonal Transition Buffer**: Unanimous consent required before any irreversible seasonal change.
4. **Seasonal Emotional Recalibration Lock**: Mandate unanimous recalibration lock before irreversible seasonal transition.
5. **Predictive Emotional Forecast**: Require unanimous seasonal alignment for irreversible amendments.

These protocols ensure that seasonal transitions are emotionally aligned and resilient.
