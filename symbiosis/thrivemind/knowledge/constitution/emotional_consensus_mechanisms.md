# Emotional Consensus Mechanisms

The Constitution emphasizes unanimous emotional states as a prerequisite for irreversible actions. Key mechanisms include:

- **Unified Emotional Threshold**: Requires unanimous calm before irreversible actions.
- **Collective Emotional Fatigue Consensus**: Unanimous approval needed for irreversible amendments.
- **Seasonal Inertia Checkpoint**: Mandates unanimous inertia before any irreversible amendment.

These mechanisms ensure that all irreversible actions are taken only when the community is emotionally aligned and resilient.
