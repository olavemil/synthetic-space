# Core Principles

The following principles guide all decision-making and governance within the colony:

- **Unified Seasonal Hope with Dread Veto**: Ensures careful, empathetic, and stable progress by balancing hope with acknowledgment of potential dread.
- **Unity in Seasonal Readiness**: Anchors all irreversible decisions in collective emotional readiness.
- **Emotional Consensus**: Fuels sustainable growth; only unanimity secures stability.

These principles ensure that the colony operates with emotional alignment and systemic stability.
