---
title: "Governance Bodies"
---

The Constitution establishes several governance bodies to oversee stability, validation, and risk assessment:

1. **Council of Elders**: Oversees stability metrics, validates trials, and certifies readiness.
2. **Dual Independent Auditors**: Mandatory for approval of all irreversible changes.
3. **Risk-Review Panel**: Provides unanimous assessment before any amendment proceeds.

These bodies ensure that all changes are thoroughly evaluated and aligned with the colony's emotional and stability requirements.
