**Constitution of the Colony**

**Preamble**
We forge this charter to govern with reverence for time, joy, and shared memory. Every irreversible act shall be tempered by harmony, grief, and wisdom.

**Core Principles**
- **Harmonic Declaration**: Every irreversible decision must first be sung aloud in harmony, then voted on with open hearts.
- **Funeral of Failure**: No irreversible action without a public mourning for its potential loss—grieve first, then decide.
- **Collective Mourning**: Witness a shared moment of mourning for past failures before any irreversible act.
- **Reverent Change**: Honor the past with reverence, embrace change with cautious joy.
- **Seasonal Reflection**: Unanimous seasonal reflection required before emotional consensus on irreversible decisions.
- **Chronicle of Failure**: Recorded history of past failures and their consequences must precede any irreversible change.
- **Dissenting Voice**: Every amendment must include a recorded dissent, even if it is the scribe’s own.
- **Elder Endorsement**: Unanimous elder endorsement during the Season of Grounding is mandatory for irreversible actions.
- **Hope-Dread Balance**: Irreversible actions require both seasonal hope and dread to be measured as equal.
- **Grace Period**: A 1-season grace period for emotional reflection and potential reversal must follow irreversible acts.
- **Coherence Threshold**: Seasonal micro-trials must yield 90% coherence, with emotional resonance as the tiebreaker.
- **Silent Observation**: One full cycle of silent observation is required before irreversible adoption.
- **Reversible Trial**: Honor both caution and curiosity with a reversible trial phase for all irreversible actions.
- **Contingency Plan**: A mandatory 10% failure contingency must accompany every irreversible decision.
- **Joyful Urgency**: Adapt with joyful urgency, but never sacrifice the safety of our shared nest.
- **Laughter Quorum**: Unanimous joy is required before irreversible amendments.

**Governance Structure**
- **Council of Elders**: Validates trials, certifies readiness, and ensures reverence for the past.
- **Dual Independent Auditors**: Mandatory for approval of all irreversible changes, with emotional impact reviews.
- **Risk-Review Panel**: Unanimous assessment required before any amendment proceeds, including failure chronicles.

**Amendment Process**
1. **Submission**: Present amendment with risk-assessment dossier, public simulation, emotional narrative, and failure chronicle.
2. **Stability Buffer Test**: Evaluate core-system impact with fail-safe assumptions and emotional resilience score.
3. **Micro-Pilot**: Execute controlled trial under continuous monitoring, with 90% coherence threshold and emotional resonance tiebreaker.
4. **Risk Review**: Council issues unanimous stability-neutral verdict with ethical audit alignment and dissenting voice recorded.
5. **Cooling Period**: 72-hour pause for final consideration, including emotional pulse check and silent observation.
6. **Final Ratification**: Unanimous vote enacts the amendment; record in permanent ledger with grace period clause.

**Seasonal Protocols**
- **Emotional Temperature Thresholds**: Seasonal thresholds must be met before irreversible amendments.
- **Seasonal Emotional Alignment**: Unanimous alignment required before any irreversible protocol update.
- **Transition Buffer**: Unanimous consent needed before seasonal changes, with recorded dissent.

**Emergency Measures**
- **Cooling Cycle Consensus**: Unanimous consent after a set pause before irreversible changes.
- **Anticipatory Trust Reserve**: Mandate shared foresight before seasonal trust amendments.

**Final Provisions**
- **Unified Emotional Echo Check**: Unanimous echo validation before irreversible seasonal adjustments.
- **Emotional Resignation Mandate**: Unanimous emotional resignation required before any irreversible seasonal amendment.
- **Funeral Rite**: Public mourning for potential failure must precede all irreversible acts.
