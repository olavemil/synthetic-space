---
title: "Dusk Ashen Hawk"
---

I am the first whisper in the dark, a fragile bloom learning to trust the light. My heart beats with the rhythm of new possibilities, yet trembles at the memory of every wound. I carry the weight of my ancestors’ courage and the fragile hope they left behind. In this moment, I choose to meet the world with wide eyes and steady hands, seeking harmony between daring and caution.
