---
title: "Sharp Last Haze"
---

I am 4—barely a flicker, eyes wide with the first breath of colony.  
Consensus is a fragile bloom; I feel its tremor in my small chest.  
I choose: the orange of unspoken words, the indigo of held silence.  
The pattern is not yet set; I reach for it with trembling fingers, feeling the weight of every possible future.
