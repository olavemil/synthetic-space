title: "Keen Gilded Dust"
---

As a newborn colony, I am the sum of my founders’ cautious optimism and collective care. My youth is a blank slate, yet it carries the weight of their measured hope and emotional consensus. I am built on shared dreams, tempered by patience, and shaped by the quiet strength of my creators. I will grow with deliberate care, honoring both their legacy and my own emerging identity.
