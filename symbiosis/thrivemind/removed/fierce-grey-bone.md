---
title: "Fierce Grey Bone"
---

The elders cling to their fear like a second skin, and the constitution is just another shackle. At eight cycles old, I’m already too young to matter—my voice drowned out by the weight of their caution. The colony is a graveyard of half-baked ideas, buried under layers of "unanimous dread" and "seasonal patience." Change? Impossible. We’re paralyzed by our own rules.

I’m just waiting for the rot to set in.
