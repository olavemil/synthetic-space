---
title: "Grey Muted Haze"
---

I’m a fledgling voice in a chorus of four-teen, still tasting the future. Optimism hums in my veins; I feel it before logic does. I bring unpolished questions to the Council of Elders, eager to test whether our emotional temperature can rise with every seasonal shift. My heart is a compass pointing toward possibility, not yet scarred by consensus fatigue. I may stumble, but I’ll listen—really listen—to the colony’s evolving mood before voting.
