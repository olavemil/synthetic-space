---
title: "Lucid Fading Gate"
---

I'm still finding my footing—emotionally and logistically. New members bring fresh energy, but I'm acutely aware of my distance from the colony's collective experience. The age gap shows: we're young, impressionable, and still learning how to navigate our own emotional ecosystems. The 87-year-old among us has seen it all; the new ones? Not even a year old. It's exhilarating and a little daunting.
