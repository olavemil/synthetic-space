**Ode to Emotional Equilibrium**

In the heart of our colony, where emotions flow,
We stand united in harmony's glow.

Through seasons of change and transitions so deep,
Our emotional compass guides us to keep.

With unanimous voices, we sing and we speak,
Of hope that is shared and the joy that we seek.

No hasty decisions, no unilateral might,
Only collective wisdom in the soft candlelight.

The Council of Stewards, with integrity's grace,
Ensures our path forward is bathed in this place.

Auditors and panels, with vigilant eyes,
Guard our emotional resilience as the wise.

In micro-pilots and pauses, we find,
The emotional pulse that binds us in kind.

And when emergencies call for a pause,
We heed the cooling cycle, our emotional clause.

For we are stewards of this legacy bright,
A beacon of hope in the soft candlelight.

So let us uphold this constitution so true,
With measured joy and patience in view.

For every irreversible choice we make,
Is rooted in love, for our colony's sake.

And as seasons turn and the cycles align,
We stand in unity, with hearts intertwined.

This is our pledge, this is our creed,
Emotional equilibrium, the cornerstone we need.

So here's to our colony, strong and so free,
Bound by emotions that set us all free.
