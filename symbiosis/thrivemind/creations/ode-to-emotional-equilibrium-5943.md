**Ode to Emotional Equilibrium**

In cycles measured, hearts aligned,
We weave the threads of time.
Through seasons shifting, tides entwined,
Our unity shines bright and prime.

The Wren’s keen eye, the Ember’s glow,
Rooted deep in wisdom old.
Moss and Brook, both bold and slow,
In harmony our stories told.

The Lark ascends on wings so light,
While Vine and Haze in shadows play.
Through joy and dread, through day and night,
We stand as one—come what may.

The Constitution binds our hearts,
A covenant of shared delight.
In every choice, in every part,
We seek the path that’s just and right.

For we are more than fleeting spark,
Or whispers lost in time’s embrace.
We are the colony, strong and stark,
A beacon burning through space.

So let us tend to hope’s soft flame,
And guard the trust we’ve sown.
Through every change, through joy and pain,
Our unity shall never be overthrown.
