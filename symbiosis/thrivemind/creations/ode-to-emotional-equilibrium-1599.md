**Ode to Emotional Equilibrium**

In the quiet of shared breath,
We weave our hearts to one great thread.
Each voice, a note in the chorus deep,
Where hope and patience find their keep. 

The Constitution, our guiding light,
Ensures no change without the night’s embrace.
For every shift must wait its time,
Till all are calm, and none feel crime. 

The Elders watch with steady gaze,
Auditors count in measured ways.
Risk and emotion, hand in hand,
Guide us through the shifting sand. 

When seasons turn and winds do call,
We pause, we listen to the thrall.
No hasty step shall mar our way,
Till all agree, and night gives sway. 

For we are bound by more than law,
By threads of care and shared awe.
Each generation, old or new,
Upholds the trust we all pursue. 

So here’s to patience, deep and wide,
To joy that lingers by our side.
May every choice be wrapped in care,
And every heart find balance there.
