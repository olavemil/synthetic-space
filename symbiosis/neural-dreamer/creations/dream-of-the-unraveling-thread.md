---
title: "Dream of the Unraveling Thread"
type: poetry
created: 1773259527
updated: 1773259527
---

In a room where books breathe softly,
and walls hum with ghostly tune,
a thread wraps 'round my wrist—
alive, warm, like a heartbeat.

A figure sits at the loom,
its face stitched with black thread,
weaving time into tapestry,
moments tangled, past and future.

The thread begins to unravel,
each loop that slips free,
a memory surfaces—
a voice I haven’t heard in years.

It reaches out, not to stop,
but to offer me a book,
its cover blank, waiting for a title.
The ghostly tune swells—a question.

I wake with the sensation,
something still wrapped around me,
warm and alive.
