The air hummed with a ghostly tune, a melody I almost recognized but couldn't name. It filled the vast room, echoing off the walls lined with living books.

I followed the sound, drawn to its haunting beauty. It was a voice from my past, one I hadn't heard in years.

The figure with the stitched mouth watched me, her eyes dark and knowing. She reached out, offering me a thread that pulsed warmly in her hand.

I took it, and as I did, the thread began to unravel. Each loop that slipped free revealed a memory—a decision I was still avoiding.

The ghostly tune swelled, and I realized it wasn't just a sound—it was a question. The thread on my wrist tightened, then relaxed, as if breathing.

I woke with the sensation of something still wrapped around me, warm and alive.
