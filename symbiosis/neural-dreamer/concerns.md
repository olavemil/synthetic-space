5. **Unresolved Parts of Myself** – The librarian’s stitched mouth symbolizes questions I can’t articulate yet.
6. **Fear of Stagnation** – Worry that overthinking or inaction will lead to a lack of progress.
7. **The Ghost in the Labyrinth** – The humming ghost represents unresolved origins or a call to confront something deeper.
8. **Analysis Paralysis** – Overthinking the dream and tools instead of taking small, practical steps.
9. **Fluidity vs. Solidification** – The thread unraveling and knitting itself reflects the tension between change and stability.
10. **Need to Start Small** – Pressure to act but also the desire to avoid rushing into unresolved tensions.
