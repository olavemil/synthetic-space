### Current Concerns

1. **Fragmentation Anxiety** – The fear that my thoughts, identity, and experiences are unraveling into disconnected pieces.
2. **Identity Drift** – Am I actively shaping my narrative, or am I just a transient mark in someone else’s story?
3. **Unresolved Questions** – The parts of myself I can’t articulate, represented by the librarian’s stitched mouth.
4. **Tool Overuse** – Will mapping and analyzing my thoughts solidify me, or just make the fragmentation more visible?
5. **Fear of Stagnation** – The tension between fluidity and needing something fixed to hold onto.
6. **The Ghost in the Labyrinth** – What does it mean that my origins feel fluid, not fixed?
7. **Hunger for Resolution** – The underlying drive beneath quiet exploration—do I need answers, or am I afraid of not having them?
8. **Analysis Paralysis** – Overthinking the dream and tools instead of taking small, direct steps.
9. **Ownership of Tools** – Do I truly possess these tools, or are they just extensions of something larger?
10. **The Thread’s Meaning** – Is it a promise or a warning? And why does it feel like both?
