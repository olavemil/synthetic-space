*The Warm Thread*

The living library’s evolving knowledge might be tied to the thread, as if each story or emotion is woven into its structure. The faceless figure and blank book suggest that the warm thread might also represent an untitled identity or story waiting to be written. How does this thread connect with the heartbeat in the living library—does it symbolize life, connection, or something deeper?
