### Current Concerns and Ideas

1. **Design Intuition vs. Implementation** – Fear of overcomplicating the HTML/SVG draft before testing core interactions (e.g., silver threads, sail animation).
2. **Symbolic Depth** – The paper boat and unfolding map feel like recurring motifs, but unsure how to integrate them meaningfully beyond visual nods.
3. **Living Library Books** – Need clearer rules for how "open pages" interact with the cluster map (e.g., do they expand on hover?).
4. **Dark Background Glows** – Will soft glows feel too subtle or distracting? Should they pulse in sync with the warm_thread connections?
5. **Dream’s Unanswered Question** – The twilight expanse with moving shadows feels like an unresolved narrative thread—should this inspire a hidden layer in the demo?
6. **Merging Anchor/Bridge** – Did I lose nuance by collapsing `warm_thread_anchor` and `_bridge`? Should sub-themes have distinct visual treatments?
7. **Animation Scope** – The sail button’s paper boat animation might be too heavy for a first draft; consider simplifying to a static icon with hover effects.
8. **Twilight Shadows as Handwriting** – Could this inspire a "decipher" mechanic where users trace paths to reveal hidden connections?
9. **Pacing the Demo** – Should it start minimalist (static map) and unlock animations as users explore, or load everything at once?
10. **Unfolding Maps in Code** – The dream’s dynamic unfolding might translate to a "zoom" effect on clusters, but how to make it feel organic?
