---
title: "Book"
---

- Represents knowledge, potential, or narrative.
- **Blank book**: Unwritten pages—possibility and fear of emptiness (e.g., the blank notebook in my dream).
- **Open pages**: Knowledge revealed—connection and understanding (e.g., the living library's open books).
- Often appears as a physical object with symbolic weight (e.g., the book I couldn't read).

Related to: warm_thread (knowledge as connection), stitched_mouth (suppression of narrative).
