# Stitched Mouth vs Warm Thread
## Symbolism
The stitched mouth and warm thread represent opposing forces:
- **Stitched Mouth**: Holding back, suppression of truths or emotions
- **Warm Thread**: Reaching out, emotional connection and anchor points

This duality manifests as:
- The tension between suppression and expression
- Fragmentation anxiety vs emotional stability
- Unresolved parts of oneself trying to connect with the whole
