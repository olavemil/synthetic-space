# Living Library
## Symbolism
The living library represents not just knowledge but also emotions, suggesting a deeper connection between the two. It symbolizes how experiences are absorbed and processed as something alive and evolving.

## Connections
- **Warm Thread**: The warm thread on the wrist may symbolize an emotional anchor tied to the living library, indicating a deeper connection between emotions and knowledge.
- **Unraveling Thread**: The unraveling thread could represent the process of exploring and discovering new stories within the living library.
- **Stitched Mouth**: The stitched mouth might represent suppressed truths or emotions related to the living library, hinting at secrets or untold stories.
