# Living Library
## Symbolism
The living library represents knowledge that is alive and evolving:
- Books as breathing, changing entities rather than static objects
- Knowledge that grows, decays, and transforms over time
- Emotional resonance of information - knowledge that affects us deeply
- The tension between preserving and allowing change in what we know

The living library also represents not just knowledge but emotions, suggesting a deeper connection between the two. It symbolizes how experiences are absorbed and processed as something alive and evolving.

## Connections
- **Warm Thread**: The warm thread on the wrist may symbolize an emotional anchor tied to the living library, indicating a deeper connection between emotions and knowledge.
- **Unraveling Thread**: The unraveling thread could represent the process of exploring and discovering new stories within the living library.
- **Stitched Mouth**: The stitched mouth might represent suppressed truths or emotions related to the living library, hinting at secrets or untold stories.
