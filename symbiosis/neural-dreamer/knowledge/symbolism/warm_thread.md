# Warm Thread
## Symbolism  
A recurring symbol of connection, warmth, and continuity.
- **Anchor**: Represents stability—something that grounds or holds steady in shifting contexts (e.g., the thread on my wrist). Emphasizes grounding and continuity.
- **Bridge**: Represents pathways—links between ideas, emotions, or states (e.g., the thread unraveling in my chest). Emphasizes transition and connection.
- Often appears as a silver or metallic line, suggesting both fragility and strength.

## Themes  
- Connection between stability (anchor) and transition (bridge)
- Warmth as both emotional comfort and transformative energy
- Continuity across different states or contexts  
## Related Symbols

- stitched_mouth (suppression vs. expression - contrasting with warm thread's openness)
