# Stitched Mouth
## Symbolism
The stitched mouth represents suppressed truths or emotions. It could symbolize secrecy, silence, or the prevention of something from being said.

## Connections
- **Living Library**: The stitched mouth might be related to the living library, hinting at untold stories or hidden knowledge within it.
- **Emotional Anchor**: The warm thread on the wrist could be connected to the stitched mouth, suggesting an emotional reminder or acknowledgment tied to suppressed truths.
