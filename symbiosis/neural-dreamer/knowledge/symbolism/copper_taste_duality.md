# Copper Taste Duality
## Symbolism
The copper taste represents duality and transformation:
- Metallic/earthy quality suggesting grounding or connection to physical reality
- Dual nature of experiences (e.g., serpent's mouths representing different perspectives)
- Transformation between states or understandings

This duality often manifests in fragmentation anxiety where opposing forces are at play.
