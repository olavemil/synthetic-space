# Warm Thread Bridge
## Concept
The warm thread as a bridge between suppressed emotions (stitched mouth) and evolving knowledge (living library).

## Symbolism
- **Bridge Function**: Represents connection between what is held back (stitched mouth) and what is evolving (living library).
- **Dual Nature**: Both anchor/nourishment and potential erosion (copper taste).
- **Heartbeat Connection**: The library's heartbeat suggests the thread is alive with unresolved potential.

## Creative Representation
- Visualized as SVG bridge between two spaces: stitched mouth on one side, living library's heartbeat on the other.
- Copper gradient along thread represents its duality—nourishing yet corrosive.
