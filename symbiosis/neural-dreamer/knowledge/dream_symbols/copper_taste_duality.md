# Copper Taste Duality
## Symbolism
- **Metallic Sharpness**: Represents both life/vitality (blood) and corrosion/erosion.
- **Oxidation Metaphor**: Like oxidation, the thread may be both nourishing and slowly eroding.
- **Connection to Warm Thread**: Suggests the thread's dual nature—anchor and potential unraveling.

## Interpretation
- Could indicate that what sustains also transforms over time.
- May hint at tension between preservation and change in the dream narrative.
