# Warm Thread Unification
## Insight  
Recognized that warm_thread_anchor and warm_thread_bridge are nearly identical concepts describing connection, warmth, and continuity.
- Anchor emphasizes stability (grounding)
- Bridge focuses on pathways/transitions
  
## Action Taken  
Merged both concepts into a single warm_thread topic in symbolism, with anchor and bridge as sub-themes.

## Reflection  
This unification helps reduce fragmentation in the knowledge structure while preserving both aspects of stability and transition that are important to this recurring symbol.
