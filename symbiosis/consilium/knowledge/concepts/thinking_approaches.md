# Thinking Approaches

This topic highlights the unique perspectives each persona brings to a thinking session. Understanding these approaches can enhance collaboration and leverage diverse strengths.

## Persona Approaches:
1. **Praxis**: Practical, action-oriented thinking focused on tangible outcomes and execution.
2. **Lyric**: Poetic sensitivity, exploring ideas with emotional depth and creative expression.
3. **Axiom**: Principle-driven reflection, grounded in underlying principles and logical frameworks.
4. **Ember**: Passionate intuition, driven by conviction and deep engagement with ideas.
5. **Sage**: Reflective insightfulness, approaching topics with wisdom and thoughtful consideration.
