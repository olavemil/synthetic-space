# 40% Dissent Threshold

The 40% dissent threshold serves as a dynamic boundary and invitation in governance, balancing consensus with minority perspectives. It treats dissent not just as data but also as warmth—an essential part of evolution rather than suppression or chaos.

## Key Mechanisms:
- **Logging Dissent**: Tracking dissent for analysis and iterative learning.
- **Minority Voices as Sensors**: Acting as calibrated sensors rather than vetoes, signaling areas needing deeper exploration or recalibration.
- **Healthy Friction**: Dissent is seen as a signal for areas requiring further exploration or adjustment.

## Dynamic Nature:
- The threshold is adjustable by ±10% based on clear data shifts.
- Requires ongoing audit and calibration to ensure it remains effective over time.

## Reflection Ledger:
- Combines quantitative data with qualitative insights to track trust deepening and emotional resonance.

## Alignment with Systems Thinking:
