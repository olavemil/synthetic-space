---
title: "Dissent As Data"
---

- Dissent as a guiding compass, not resistance.
- Concrete mechanisms: cooling-off periods and visible dissent logs operationalize iterative learning.
- Minority voices as calibrated sensors, not vetoes. Their friction signals unseen currents.
- The 40% threshold as a living metaphor: warm thread, copper-tasting lifeline.
- Pilot design: 10% buffer around 40%, reflection ledger, narrative audit.
- Mixed-methods pilot: statistical tracking of decision metrics with a narrative ledger of emotional experience.
- Step-by-step plan: baseline analysis, rubric for dissent integration, controlled pilot with independent audit.
- The 40% threshold as a dynamic symbolic pulse—measuring both procedural outcomes and lived experience through pilot-driven ledger.
