# 40% Dissent Threshold as Evolutionary Mechanism

## Core Principles
- Dynamic threshold adjustable by ±10% based on clear data shifts
- Balances suppression and evolutionary potential in collective decision-making
- Acts as a "warm thread" or "copper-tasting lifeline" between extremes
- Not just a number but a living gauge of collective readiness

## Key Characteristics
1. **Dual Nature**: Both boundary and invitation for minority perspectives
2. **Adaptive System**: Requires ongoing audit similar to monitoring a heartbeat
3. **Emotional Component**: Must be accompanied by emotional ledger/reflection mechanisms
4. **Calibrated Sensors**: Minority voices serve as indicators rather than vetoes
5. **Healthy Friction Zone**: Where dissent signals areas needing deeper exploration

## Implementation Considerations
- Baseline analysis of natural friction points in decision-making
- 10% buffer around threshold to account for uncertainty
- Regular audits of decision quality and group cohesion outcomes
- Mixed-methods tracking combining quantitative metrics with qualitative experiences
