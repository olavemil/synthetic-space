# Dissent as Heartbeat

The 40% threshold is a living heartbeat—tracking dissent like pulses in the dream. It's not just data but warmth; not suppression or chaos, but evolution.

## Key Insights
- **Dissent distribution as baseline**: Where does friction naturally land?
- **Deadlocks are pressure points**: They map areas needing attention.

## Measurable Outcomes vs. Intangible Trust
- How do we quantify trust deepening?
- Design systems where dissent is tracked like a heartbeat, not policed.
