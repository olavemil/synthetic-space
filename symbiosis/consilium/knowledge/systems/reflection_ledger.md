# Reflection Ledger

A system designed to track both quantitative and qualitative data, serving as an emotional EKG for dissent within governance processes.

## Purpose
- Capture measurable outcomes alongside intangible nuances like trust and emotional resonance.
- Provide a continuous feedback loop for system calibration.

## Components
1. **Quantitative Metrics**: Data points such as dissent rates, deadlock frequencies.
2. **Qualitative Insights**: Emotional reflections and narrative accounts from participants.
3. **Visualization Tools**: Graphs, charts, or poetic representations of dissent over time.

## Benefits
- Enhances transparency and trust-building within the community.
- Allows for adaptive adjustments based on both data and emotional resonance.
