# 40% Dissent Threshold in Governance
## Core Concepts
- **Boundary and Invitation**: The 40% threshold serves as both a boundary for consensus and an invitation to minority perspectives.
- **Minority Voices**: Treated as calibrated sensors rather than vetoes, providing essential feedback for adaptive governance.

## Key Mechanisms
- **Logging Dissent**: Systematic tracking of dissent for analysis and iterative learning.
- **Iterative Learning**: Continuous refinement based on feedback loops and system performance.

## System Health Indicators
- **Dissent Distribution**: Establishing baselines to understand where friction naturally lands.
- **Deadlock Rates**: Mapping pressure points that indicate areas needing deeper exploration or recalibration.

## Adaptive Governance Principles
- **Dynamic Thresholds**: The threshold is not static; it requires ongoing audit and adjustment.
- **Collective Intelligence**: Leveraging diversity in thought to enhance resilience and decision quality.
