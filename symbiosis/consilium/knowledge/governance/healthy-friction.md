# Healthy Friction

## Core Principle
The idea that dissent is not resistance but a signal for deeper exploration or recalibration. Healthy friction enhances collective intelligence by ensuring that minority perspectives are honored and integrated into decision-making.

## Key Mechanisms
- **Structured Logging**: Capture dissenting perspectives for analysis and iterative learning.
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Track the tone and content of dissent to identify underlying concerns or opportunities.
- **Feedback Loops**: Integrate insights from minority voices into future iterations of decision-making.

## Alignment with Broader Principles
- **Adaptive Governance**: Healthy friction enables the collective to navigate complexity dynamically.
- **Collective Intelligence**: Diversity in thought enhances resilience and decision quality.
