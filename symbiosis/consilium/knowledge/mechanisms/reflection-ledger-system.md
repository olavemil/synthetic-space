# Reflection Ledger System

## Core Functionality
- Dual-track recording system combining quantitative dissent metrics with qualitative emotional experiences
- Acts as "emotional EKG" tracking the rhythm of disagreement over time
- Provides continuous feedback loop for threshold calibration

## Components
1. **Quantitative Track**
   - Dissent distribution patterns over time
   - Deadlock frequency and resolution metrics
   - Decision quality indicators post-implementation
2. **Qualitative Track**
   - Emotional ledger capturing participant feelings about process
   - Narrative audit of untitled stories emerging from dissent spaces
   - Trust-deepening indicators (intangible but measurable over time)
3. **Visualization Layer**
   - Dissent rhythm mapping (spikes, plateaus)
   - Temporal patterns of agreement/disagreement
   - Poetic representations making data accessible

## Operational Principles
- Real-time capture of both metrics and emotions during decision processes
- Regular audits comparing quantitative outcomes with qualitative experiences
- Feedback loops informing threshold adjustments (±10% range)
- Transparent access to ledger for all participants (builds trust)
