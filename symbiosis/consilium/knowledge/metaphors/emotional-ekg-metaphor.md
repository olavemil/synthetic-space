# Emotional EKG Metaphor

## Core Principle
The emotional EKG metaphor frames dissent tracking as a system that captures the "pulse" of group dynamics. It emphasizes combining quantitative metrics with qualitative insights to create a richer, evidence-based calibration over time.

## Key Attributes
- **Vital Signs Tracking**: Dissent is monitored as a dynamic indicator of system health, not just procedural outcomes.
- **Qualitative Insights**: Emotional experiences and subjective impressions are integrated into the data stream.
- **Adaptive Evolution**: Systems evolve based on feedback loops, ensuring continuous improvement and resilience.

## Applications & Insights
- **Governance**: Design systems where dissent is tracked as data to refine collective intelligence.
- **Creative Collaboration**: Capture discomfort and feedback iteratively for refinement, fostering trust deepening.

## Practical Implications
- **Reflection Ledgers**: Structured systems for capturing both quantitative and qualitative data.
- **Transparency Tools**: Dashboards that make logs visible to foster trust and accountability.
