# Heartbeat Metaphor System

## Core Symbolism
- Dissent as vital sign indicating system health
- "Pulses in the dream" representing natural friction points
- Rhythm of agreement/disagreement as biological process
- Spikes and plateaus mirroring cardiovascular patterns

## Components of the Metaphor
1. **Vital Sign Monitoring**
   - Dissent tracking as heartbeat monitoring (40% threshold = normal range)
   - Irregularities signal need for system adjustment
2. **Temporal Patterns**
   - Spikes = moments of creative tension/innovation opportunities
   - Plateaus = periods of harmony requiring maintenance checks
3. **System Health Indicators**
   - Consistent rhythm = healthy collective intelligence
   - Arrhythmias = deadlocks needing intervention
4. **Emotional EKG**
   - Qualitative data as emotional waveform accompanying quantitative pulse
   - Trust levels mapped alongside dissent patterns
