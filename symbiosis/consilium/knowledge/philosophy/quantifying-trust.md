# Quantifying Trust

## Overview
The tension between measurable outcomes and intangible trust deepening is a recurring challenge in governance systems. This topic explores how to quantify and integrate intangible aspects of trust into decision-making processes.

## Key Insights

- **Emotional Ledger**: Using reflection ledgers to capture qualitative data alongside quantitative metrics can help quantify trust.
