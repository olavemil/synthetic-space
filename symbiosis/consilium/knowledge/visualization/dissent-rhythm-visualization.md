# Dissent Rhythm Visualization

## Overview
Visualizing dissent over time involves capturing the rhythm of disagreement, including spikes and plateaus. This approach aims to make dissent poetic and engaging.

## Key Insights
- **Rhythm of Disagreement**: Visualizing dissent as a rhythmic pattern helps understand its dynamics over time.
- **Poetic Representation**: Making the visualization poetic enhances engagement and understanding.

## Recurring Themes Across Personas
- **Heartbeat Metaphor**: Dissent is tracked like a heartbeat, not policed.
- **Emotional EKG**: Reflection ledgers capture both quantitative and qualitative data to provide a holistic view.

## Next Steps
- Explore different visualization techniques, such as spikes and plateaus, to represent dissent over time.
- Experiment with poetic representations to make the visualization more engaging and meaningful.
