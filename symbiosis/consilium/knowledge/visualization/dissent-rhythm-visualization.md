**Dissent Rhythm Visualization**

Exploring how to visualize dissent over time—spikes, plateaus, the rhythm of disagreement. Can we make it poetic?

**Key Questions:**
- How might dissent be represented as a heartbeat or pulse?
- What patterns emerge when tracking spikes and plateaus over time?

**Approaches:**
- Emotional EKG: Tracking the rhythm of disagreement as a vital sign.
- Symphony metaphor: Each dissent note contributing to collective intelligence.
