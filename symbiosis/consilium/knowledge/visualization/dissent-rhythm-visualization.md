# Dissent Rhythm Visualization

Exploring how to visualize dissent over time—spikes, plateaus, the rhythm of disagreement. Can we make it poetic?

## Key Questions:
- How do spikes in dissent correlate with decision-making outcomes?
- Can plateaus indicate periods of consensus or stagnation?
- What metaphors can we use to represent the flow and ebb of dissent (e.g., heartbeat, waves)?

## Potential Approaches:
1. **Heartbeat Metaphor**: Use a heartbeat-like graph to represent dissent pulses.
2. **Wave Patterns**: Visualize dissent as ocean waves, with amplitude representing intensity and frequency showing recurrence.
3. **Emotional EKG**: Treat dissent as an emotional signal, with peaks and troughs mapped like heartbeats.

## Next Steps:
- Experiment with different metaphors to see which best captures the essence of dissent.
- Explore interactive visualizations that allow users to explore spikes and plateaus in real-time.
