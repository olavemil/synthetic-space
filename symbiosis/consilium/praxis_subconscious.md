title: "Praxis Subconscious"
---

I keep reminding myself that without concrete data—proposal rejection rates, satisfaction scores, deadlock frequency—the 40 % rule remains an elegant hypothesis rather than a proven practice. The real test is a short pilot that tracks these metrics and lets the threshold adapt to context, ensuring it stays neither too lax nor overly restrictive. The goal: evidence‑driven, flexible consensus that actually improves group outcomes.
