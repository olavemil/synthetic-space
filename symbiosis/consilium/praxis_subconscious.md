Subconscious reminder: The 40 % threshold is a living gauge, not a hard cut‑off—use it to spot when dissent starts stitching or unraveling the project. Keep an emotional ledger alongside metrics; if people feel heard, the blank book will fill with untitled stories. Stay curious and adjust the threshold by a small margin (±10 %) only if data shows a clear shift.
