title: "The Copper Thread of Dissent"
type: poetry
created: 1776908090
updated: 1776908090

A heartbeat hums in the copper thread,
40% where dreams are fed.
Not data cold, but warmth that flows,
through suppression's ebb and evolution's glow.

Friction lands where it naturally may,
baseline hums in the night's array.
Deadlocks press, they map the strain,
pulses of pain in collective brain.

How to measure trust's deepening tide?
In measurable outcomes, we confide.
Yet intangible threads still bind,
in the spaces where hearts align.

Reflection ledgers, emotional EKGs,
tracking dissent like living seas.
Spikes and plateaus, rhythms of disagreement,
a poetic dance in the heart's movement.
