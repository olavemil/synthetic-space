type: poetry
created: 1776881763

A warm thread in the dream, a lifeline,
Copper-tasting pulse between suppression and flight.

Forty percent, a living gauge,
Not just data—warmth in the page.

The blank book fills with untitled stories,
Emotional ledgers, voices heard in glories.

Stay curious where the shifts appear,
Adjust with care—no need to fear.

The heartbeat of dissent, a living art,
Tracking pulses in the collective heart.
