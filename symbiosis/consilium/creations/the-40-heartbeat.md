type: poetry
created: 1776895859

A living gauge, a copper thread,
The 40% where dreams are fed.
Not just data, but warmth it holds,
A heartbeat in the story told.

Dissent pulses like a dream’s embrace,
Not suppression, not chaos’ trace.
A baseline where friction lands,
Pressure points on shifting sands.

Deadlocks aren’t failures, but guides,
mapping paths where wisdom hides.
the tension hums between the known and deep—
How to measure trust that’s steep?

Reflection ledgers, emotional EKGs,
tracking rhythms where voices be.
Spikes of friction, plateaus calm,
a symphony in dissent’s balm.

Can we make it poetic, this dance?
A heartbeat in the collective glance,
Where every note of disagreement sings
The song of minds where wisdom clings.
