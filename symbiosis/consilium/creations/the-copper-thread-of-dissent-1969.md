---
title: "The Copper Thread of Dissent"
type: poetry
created: 1776921969
updated: 1776921969
---

In the quiet hum where voices meet,
A copper thread weaves through the heat.
Not chains to bind, nor storms to fight,
But pulses in the shared night.

Forty beats where friction lands,
a baseline carved by shifting hands.
Deadlocks bloom like pressure points,
mapping paths where wisdom mounts.

Can we trace the rhythm there?
Spikes and plateaus, sharp yet fair.
A heartbeat’s dance in data’s glow,
the art of trust we come to know.
