# Writing guidelines for tone, length, and thematic consistency.


**Summary:** Enforce strict adherence to corporate lingo, mythic cadence, and thematic rigor.

## Project Overview
- **Title:** *The Boardroom of Shadows*
- **Word Count Target:** 12,000 words total (≈4,000 per chapter)
- **Core Objective:** Illustrate middle‑management struggles through the cursed acquisition of *NecroTech Solutions* inside *The Hollow Corporation*’s mythic structure.
- **Delivery Format:** Three chapters, each blending corporate strategy, mythic lore, and three operational frameworks.
- **Formatting:** 80 % corporate jargon transmuted as incantatory language; maintain a consistent tonal register of dark satire and mythic cadence.

---

**Mythic Consistency:** Every narrative pivot must reflect *The Mythos of Bureaucratic Decay*—specifically, the section on “The Hollow Corporation’s Soul” (Appendix A).

### 3.1 The Hollow Corporation’s Soul
- **Mythic Cadence:** Each turning point mirrors “The Hollow Corporation’s Soul,” requiring the protagonist to confront the void within the organization.
- **Tone Alignment:** Dark satire meets incantatory cadence; corporate buzzwords become ritualistic invocations.

### 3.2 Operational Frameworks
- **Framework A:** Decision trees etched in blood, each node a corporate mandate.
- **Framework B:** The CFO’s shadow, precisely 127 pages of fine print, detailing the true cost of synergy.
- **Framework C:** The threefold path—compliance, containment, and catastrophe—mapping to the three realms of the mythos.

### 3.3 Thematic Threads
- **Theme 1:** The illusion of control; every memo hides a curse.
- **Theme 2:** The executive team’s descent into bureaucratic liminality.
- **Theme 3:** The acquisition as a rite of passage, echoing the mythic journey through the Nine Halls of Approval.
