## Project Overview
The Hollow Corporation, Q3 2024. Post-merger with NecroTech Solutions. Executive team haunted by ledgers and regret.

### Chapter 1: The Ledger’s Echo
**Setting:** The Glass Atrium, where the CFO’s silhouette flickers like a failing server.
**Key Action:** Middle management must decode the *Codex of Forgotten Budgets*, a 400-page manual bound in human skin.
**Frameworks:**
1. **Triad of Accountability** – A ritual where VPs sign binding contracts under candlelight.
2. **The Paradox of Overlapping Authority** – Middle managers assign tasks to themselves, creating recursive loops.
3. **The Audit of Echoes** – Monthly reviews where every email is replayed through a crystal ball.

### Chapter 2: The Casualty
**Setting:** The War Room, a circular table with no head.
**Key Action:** The CFO discovers the *Balance Sheet* – a living document that consumes dissenters.
**Frameworks:**
1. **Three-P-Step Hemorrhage** – Cut costs, cut staff, cut corners; the cycle repeats.
2. **The Mandate of Obscurity** – Policies written in invisible ink; only the CFO can read them.
3. **The Ritual of Redundancy** – Mandatory cross-departmental meetings where everyone agrees to disagree.

### Chapter 3: The Reckoning
**Setting:** The Vault, where old financials are entombed in ice.
**Key Action:** A hostile bidder arrives with a single question: “What is the cost of silence?”
**Frameworks:**
1. **The Law of Unintended Consequences** – Every loophole births a new crisis.
2. **The Doctrine of the Frozen Ledger** – A policy that freezes salaries until audits are complete.
3. **The Edict of Echoing Commissions** – All bonuses must be spoken aloud to the board.

**Appendix A: Mythos of Bureaucratic Decay**
- *The Hollow Corporation* – A corporation whose soul is a merger with NecroTech Solutions.
- *The CFO* – Embodiment of ledger guilt, bound to the balance sheet.
- *Synergy* – A blood pact that collapses under its own weight.

**Appendix B: Management Frameworks**
- *Triad of Accountability* – A ritual requiring three signatories.
- *Audit of Echoes* – A monthly review of every email.
- *Mandate of Obscurity* – Policies written in invisible ink.

**Appendix C: Corporate Mythos**
- *Hostile Takeover* – A ritual where the weak are culled.
- *Middle Management* – The liminal space between power and irrelevance.
- *Hostage Negotiation* – Middle managers negotiate their own survival.
