# Writing Style Guide

## Tone & Voice
- **Formal yet lyrical**: Corporate jargon repurposed as mythic incantation (e.g., "synergy" = blood pact). Maintain dark satire with mythic cadence.
- **Tonal register**: Blend of bureaucratic precision and ritualistic weight. Example: "The quarterly report was not merely data—it was an offering to the Hollow’s appetite."

## Structural Rules
- **Chapter length**: 3800–4200 words per chapter (including whitespace).
- **Framework integration**: Each chapter embeds three operational frameworks, each with:
  - Core tenets (3–5 bullet points)
  - Implementation checklist
- **Synergy-pact clause**: Exactly one per chapter, ≤150 characters. Format: `[Framework A] + [Framework B] = [Mythic Outcome]` (e.g., "Decision-Tree Acquisition + Triad of Accountability = The Hollow’s Approval").

## Glyph Mapping
- **Single semantic field**: Each glyph maps to one corporate concept (e.g., ⚡ = synergy, 🔒 = compliance).
- **Consistency**: Glyphs repeat across chapters for recurring themes (e.g., 🏛️ = The Hollow Corporation).

## Corporate-Mythic Translations
- **Terms**: Convert 80% of corporate terms into mythic equivalents (e.g., "KPI" → "Soul Metric").
- **Dialogue**: Executives speak in ritualistic cadence (e.g., "The board’s decision echoes through the Nine Halls of Approval").

## Example
**Corporate**: "We need to align our KPIs with the quarterly targets."
**Mythic**: "The Soul Metrics must resonate within Q4’s echo chambers."

## Verification
- Every claim is specific (e.g., "The Atrium’s 12 lux illumination" vs. "dim lighting").
- No abstract generalizations (e.g., avoid "the company’s culture" without concrete examples).

## Whitespace
- Paragraphs: 3–5 sentences max.
- Line breaks between sections for rhythm.

## Endnotes
1. Glyphs are reserved symbols; no emoji substitutions.
2. Frameworks must link to mythic tenets (Appendix A).
3. Synergy-pact clauses are verbatim in Appendix B.
