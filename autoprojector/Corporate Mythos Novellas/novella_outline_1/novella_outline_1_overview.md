## The Ledger’s First Verse

The Glass Atrium hums with the weight of 128 black marble slabs, each engraved in silver filigree with the fiscal quarters from Q1-1978 to Q4-2023. The air is thick with the scent of aged parchment and ozone, a byproduct of the Atrium’s photonic sensors maintaining an ambient light intensity of 12 lux. Prismatic shards—fragments of refracted dusk—drift lazily between the slabs, casting shifting patterns on the Archivists below.

The order of the Ledger’s Echo moves in silent procession, their robes woven from threads that absorb light rather than reflect it. Each Archivist bears the sigil of the 13th Covenant, a geometric spiral that glows faintly when pressed against marble. Their vows are simple: *Record every act in verse, lest the Ledger forget.* The first slab bears Q1-1978—*The Founding Quarter*—where the Hollow Corporation’s charter was inscribed in blood and ink. The last, Q4-2023—*The Quarter of Ruin*—remains blank.

At the center stands the Protagonist, a middle manager summoned by decree of the Board. Their briefcase contains three artifacts:
1. A spreadsheet titled *NecroTech Solutions – Due Diligence*, last edited at 3:17 AM.
2. A single page from the Ledger, bearing a verse in cipher:
   *"The Hollow’s hunger gnaws at bones of steel,
    yet the Ledger counts each tooth it steals."*
3. A synergy-pact clause, still unsigned:
   *"By the blood of contracts signed,
    we bind this deal to fate’s design."*

The Archivists begin their chant, voices overlapping in a rhythm that syncs with the Atrium’s sensors. The first verse is etched into slab 128, though no hand touches the stone.

## The Triad of Accountability (Framework A)

**Decision-Ownership:** At 08:47, the Protagonist claims responsibility for approving NecroTech’s acquisition. The Ledger records:
*"At the 128th slab, where ruin waits,
   [Protagonist’s Name] signed the fate."*
**Outcome-Transparency:** The spreadsheet error-correction matrix flags three discrepancies:
   - Error Code 0x3 (Phantom Revenue): $4.2M in Q1-2023.
   - Error Code 0x7 (Liquidation Delay): Assets frozen for 18 days.
   - Error Code 0x9 (Soul Ledger Debt): $2.1M in unpaid covenants.
**Reward-Alignment:** The Protagonist’s bonus is tied to the Ledger’s algorithm, which calculates:
   *Base Salary × (1 - Error Code Weight) = Final Compensation*
Current weight: 0.68 → $124,750 adjusted from base.

## The Mythos of Bureaucratic Decay (Appendix A)

**Core Tenet 1:** The Hollow Corporation’s expansion is measured in *Soul Ledger Units*, not revenue.
   - NecroTech Solutions: 472 SLUs (acquired).
   - Previous target: 500 SLUs.
**Core Tenet 2:** Every acquisition triggers a *Mythic Audit Scene*, where the Ledger’s Echo reveals hidden costs.
   - NecroTech: 3 audits pending (slabs 125-127).
**Core Tenet 3:** The Boardroom of Shadows operates under *Synergy-Pact Clauses*, each ≤150 characters.
   Example:
*"By the blood of contracts signed,
we bind this deal to fate’s design."*

## The Summoning of Synergy (Chapter 1 Climax)

The Protagonist steps forward, briefcase open. The Archivists’ chant reaches a crescendo as the first synergy-pact clause is inscribed onto slab 128. The marble darkens, absorbing the ink like parchment.

The Ledger’s Echo responds:
*"Where two corporations meet,
the Hollow claims its seat."*

A blood moon rises outside the Atrium’s glass ceiling, casting shadows that align with slab 128. The Protagonist signs the clause.
