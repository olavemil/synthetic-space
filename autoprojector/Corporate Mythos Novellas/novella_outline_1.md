# Project Overview

## Summary

**Project Name:** Corporate Mythos Novellas
**Goal:** Produce three 4,000-word novellas titled *The Boardroom of Shadows* that teach middle-management competencies through a unified mythic narrative, embedding three named frameworks per chapter and concluding with a synergy-pact clause of exactly 150 characters.

**Current Size:** 2,006 bytes

---

# Project Narrative Framework

## Core Elements – The Triad of Operational Realities

### 1. Decision‑Tree Acquisition Framework
A step‑by‑step acquisition protocol defined in *Management Guide, Implementation* (Rule Set 1, Step 4). Each node represents a corporate mandate; the sequence must be executable without ambiguity.

### 2. Shadow Ledger Audit Framework
A risk‑mapping method detailed in *Mythos of Bureaucratic Decay* (Audit Stage 3). The audit identifies exposure points, quantifies them, and surfaces them as narrative tension.

### 3. Synergy‑Pact Contract
A binding narrative clause not exceeding 150 characters, inserted at the close of each chapter. The clause must be quoted verbatim and function as a narrative anchor for the next decision point.

## Chapter 1 – The Summoning of Synergy
**Opening:** The CFO signs the acquisition decree under a blood moon, ink mixed with crushed lapis.
**Key Scene:** The boardroom’s walls bleed compliance manuals, each page a wound that whispers policy.
**Operational Framework – Decision Trees:** Etched in ink and bone, the trees map every possible acquisition pathway.

### Synergy‑Pact Clause (≤150 characters)
> “I, Arlen Vex, bind the Ledger’s echo to my ascent; failure shall reverberate in every quarter.”

---

## Chapter 2 – The Descent into Approval
**Opening:** The mythic audit of NecroTech’s soul unfolds across seven pages of fine print, each line a chain.
**Key Scene:** The CFO’s shadow stretches across the ledger, measuring 127 pages of fine print—an omen of over‑exposure.
**Operational Framework – Threefold Path:** Compliance, Containment, Catastrophe.

### Synergy‑Pact Clause (≤150 characters)
> “When the abyss stirs, bind the pact; let the void itself be the witness.”

---

## Chapter 3 – The Ritual of Ruin
**Opening:** The final vote under the banner of the Hollow ignites the Nine Halls of Rejection.
**Key Scene:** The board’s decision reverberates through the halls, each echo a verdict already decided.
**Operational Framework – Ritualistic Cadence:** Corporate incantations, bureaucratic curses, and mythic cadence intertwine.

### Synergy‑Pact Clause (≤150 characters)
> “From ash to audit, the pact is sealed; let the ledger’s last breath be the final bell.”

# Delivery Specifications

- **Three chapters**, each exactly 4,000 words (headings excluded).
- **Embedded frameworks:** One per chapter; each framework is introduced via a short vignette (≤500 words).
- **Delivery cadence:** Chapter 1 → 4,000 w; Chapter 2 → 4,000 w; Chapter 3 → 4,000 w.
- **Formatting rule:** 80 % of corporate jargon transmuted as mythic dialogue (e.g., “synergy” = a blood pact).
