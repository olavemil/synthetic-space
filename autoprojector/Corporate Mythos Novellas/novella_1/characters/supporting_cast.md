**Name:** Liora Veyne

**Bio:**
Liora is Elias’s younger sister, a scholar of atmospheric control in The Glass Atrium. She specializes in the manipulation of light and sound to preserve historical echoes, though her work is often overshadowed by the political weight of the Ledger’s Echo.

**Background:**
Liora was trained under Master Kael, an esteemed architect of the Atrium’s environmental systems. Unlike her brother, she never sought a role in the Triad of Accountability—her focus was on preserving the *feeling* of history, not its record. When Elias’s exile occurred, she used her influence to delay the erasure of his name from official ledgers. Now, she secretly aids him in uncovering truths the Triad would rather keep buried.

**Arc:**
Liora’s journey is one of quiet rebellion. She begins as a loyal servant to the Atrium, but her growing disillusionment with its rigid hierarchy drives her toward Elias’s cause. Her arc culminates in a choice: either restore the Atrium to its original purpose or help dismantle it entirely.

**Appearances:**
- *Chapter 1*: Introduced as a distant figure observing Elias’s return.
- *Chapter 3*: Reveals her knowledge of the altered ledger entry that exiled Elias.
- *Chapter 5*: Leads him through hidden passages to access restricted atmospheric controls.
