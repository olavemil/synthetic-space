**Name:** Elias Veyne

**Bio:**
Elias is a former archivist of the Glass Atrium, now an exiled scribe seeking redemption. His expertise in translating stanzas into glyphs is unmatched, but his past actions have left him haunted by the echoes of forgotten acts.

**Background:**
Elias was once a trusted member of the Triad, responsible for recording and preserving acts within The Glass Atrium. His meticulous work ensured the integrity of the Ledger’s Echo, but a single misstep—altering an entry to protect someone he loved—led to his downfall. Now, stripped of his title and banished from the Atrium’s inner chambers, he wanders its periphery as a ghost of his former self.

**Arc:**
Elias’s journey is one of atonement. He must confront the consequences of his past actions, not only to reclaim his place but also to uncover a deeper truth about the Atrium’s guardians. His arc hinges on whether he can restore balance to the Ledger or if his guilt will consume him entirely.

**Appearances:**
- **Chapter 1:** Introduced as a shadowy figure observing the Atrium’s rituals from afar.
- **Chapter 3:** Confronts his past when a new entry in the Ledger reveals an act tied to his alteration.
- **Chapter 5:** Faces a moral dilemma that could either redeem him or seal his fate.
