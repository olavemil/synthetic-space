# Ledger’s Echo Metaphor

The phrase *"Your ledger echoes here"* functions as a narrative device to emphasize transparency and accountability within the Glass Atrium’s mythos. It literalizes the idea that every recorded act—whether a negotiation, ritual, or decision—resonates through time and space. The echo is not just a sound but an immutable record, ensuring that actions cannot be hidden or altered without consequence.

### Examples in Dialogue

1. **Elias Veyne’s Confession**
   *"Your ledger echoes here, High Custodian. The glyphs do not lie—my alteration of the nexus ritual was recorded before I could erase it."*

2. **Liora Veyne’s Warning**
   *"Speak carefully, brother. The Atrium remembers every word you’ve ever whispered in these walls."*

3. **Varek Thorn’s Rebuke**
   *"The ledger echoes your hesitation, Elias. Indecision is itself an act—and it has been logged."*

Each instance reinforces the Triad of Accountability: Authority (the act), Transparency (the echo), and Reciprocity (the inevitable reckoning). The metaphor ensures that no action goes unnoticed, framing the Atrium as both a courtroom and an archive.
