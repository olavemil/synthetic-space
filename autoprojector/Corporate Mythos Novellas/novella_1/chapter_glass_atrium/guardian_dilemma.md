# Triad Collaborative Ritual – Execution

## Overview
The Triad Collaborative Ritual binds Authority, Transparency, and Reciprocity into a single glyphic act that restores balance when a ledger entry is contested. It must be performed inside the Glass Atrium under strict atmospheric conditions.

## Preconditions
1. Atmospheric setting: *Cerulean Veil* – amber illumination tuned to a 432 Hz resonance pulse.
2. Central Glyph Nexus must be empty; no glyph may already occupy its core.
3. Presence of at least one Guardian with a Gold Quill and one with a Silver Quill.
4. The protagonist’s risk tolerance is logged; a low tolerance flag triggers a cautionary echo.

## Sequence of Actions
1. **Calibration** – Liora adjusts the Resonance Lantern to emit a 432 Hz pulse, causing the black marble slabs to vibrate at the designated frequency.
2. **Pillar Activation** – The Authority Pillar is lit with a focused white shaft, projecting the unilateral‑decree glyph onto the central slab.
3. **Inking** – The Gold Quill Guardian inscribes the primary glyph, embedding the act’s essence; the Silver Quill Guardian adds a secondary glyph that records the act’s transparency.
4. **Echo Capture** – The acoustic echo field is engaged, recording the spoken incantation as a resonant waveform within the chamber.
5. **Release** – The Obsidian Quill Guardian releases the resonance field, allowing the echo to propagate outward and seal the act within the Ledger’s Echo.

## Consequences
- Successful execution restores equilibrium; any prior distortion is nullified.
- Failure to meet precondition 1 creates a corrupted echo that amplifies dissent, invoking the Echo Chamber Paradox.
- The protagonist’s low risk tolerance is reflected in the echo, shaping future guardians’ decisions.
