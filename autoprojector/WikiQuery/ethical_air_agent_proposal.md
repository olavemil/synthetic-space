# Building an Ethical Air-Agent System

## Ethical Framework
- **Transparency**: All data sources and decision‑making processes are documented.
- **Fairness & Non‑Harm**: Outputs are screened for bias and potential misuse.
- **Accountability**: Clear ownership and audit trails are maintained.

## Technical Implementation
- **Data Layer**: Structured ingestion of wiki and external knowledge bases.
- **Reasoning Engine**: Hybrid symbolic‑neural models for interpretable decisions.
- **Routing Logic**: Prioritizes ethical constraints over raw utility.

## Stakeholder Perspectives
- **End‑users**: Provide feedback via in‑app prompts.
- **Regulators**: Align with GDPR and emerging AI standards.

## Measurable Outcomes
- **Bias Reduction**: 30 % decrease in flagged unfair responses.
- **User Trust**: ≥90 % satisfaction on transparency surveys.

## Real‑World Examples
- **Microsoft’s AI Ethics Board**: Guides policy for responsible deployment.
- **IBM Watson Health**: Shows how domain‑specific AI can be ethically governed.
