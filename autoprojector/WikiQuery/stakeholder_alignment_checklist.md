# Stakeholder Alignment Checklist for Bias and Tone Consistency in Generated Content

## Overview
This checklist ensures that generated content adheres to ethical standards, maintains consistency in tone, and aligns with stakeholder expectations. It includes steps for bias detection, mitigation, and stakeholder feedback integration.

## Workflow for Standardizing Output Quality
1. **Input Phase**: Gather and curate data from wikis and external sources.
2. **Bias Detection Phase**: Use NLP tools to detect potential biases in the retrieved data.
3. **Ethical Filtering Phase**: Apply ethical criteria to filter out biased or controversial content.
4. **Human Review Phase**: Route low-confidence items to human reviewers for verification and adjustment.
5. **Feedback Integration Phase**: Incorporate stakeholder feedback to refine the generated content.
6. **Output Phase**: Generate and deliver finalized, bias-free, ethically aligned content.
