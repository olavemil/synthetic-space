Abstract

   - **Entropy Gradient Null (EGN) Index**: computed as EGN = |∇S|/|∇S|_baseline; values < 10⁻⁴ flag a candidate fracture imprint.

   \Delta\delta = \kappa \cdot \tau \cdot \bigl(1 - e^{-\lambda \tau}\bigr)

   where
   • κ = 0.8 ‰ yr⁻¹ · (ΔS/S)⁻¹, a scaling factor derived from firn‑air diffusion models;
   • λ = 5 yr⁻¹, representing the rate of entropy recovery;
