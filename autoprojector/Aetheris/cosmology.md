Temporal Loops

Overview

They are distinguished from simple repetition by a persistent signature that survives transformations, enabling identification and prediction of their occurrence.

Loop Signature

- Curvature tensor component (C_{μν})

\mathbf{L} = (f,\;\eta,\;C_{\mu\nu},\;I)
