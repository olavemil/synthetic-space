## The Song of the Forgotten Future

The phrase pointed to a specific pattern of radio emissions—narrowband bursts at 1420 MHz, the hydrogen line, modulated by a sequence of prime numbers. The bursts were not random; they were a message, and the messengers were the universe’s oldest echoes.
