# Entropy Borrowing: The Physics of Reversed Entropy

## Core Principle
Entropy borrowing describes a localized reversal of the thermodynamic arrow of time, where entropy decreases temporarily. This phenomenon is governed by the **Entropy Gradient Inversion Condition**:

\[
\nabla S \rightarrow -\nabla S
\]

When satisfied, the system enters a reversible state with:

\[
\Delta t = \frac{\hbar}{k_B T} e^{-\beta (-\nabla S)}
\]

\[
\tau < \frac{\hbar}{k_B T} e^{-\beta (-\nabla S)}
