\[
\nabla S = \frac{\partial s}{\partial t} + \nabla \cdot (s\mathbf{v}) = 0
\]
When \(\nabla S \to 0\), the convective term disappears, reducing evolution to an algebraic constraint that yields a stationary metric \(g_{\mu\nu}\).
