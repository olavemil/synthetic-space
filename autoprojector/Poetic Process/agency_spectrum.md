Agency Mapping Framework

1. Core Entities
- **Maker**: The originator of intent, idea, or action.
- **Medium**: The channel, material, or system that interprets and transmits the maker’s input.
- **Agency**: The capacity of each to influence outcomes independently.

2. Spectrum of Agency (0–10 scale)
- **0**: Pure determinism – medium dictates outcome.
- **10**: Pure agency – maker controls outcome.
- **5**: Balanced interplay – mutual negotiation shapes result.

3. Types of Control
- **Formal**: Rules, structures, or constraints explicitly defined (e.g., word limits).
- **Thematic**: Underlying motifs guiding expression (e.g., “fragility”).
- **Dynamic**: Emergent properties arising from interaction.

5. Practical Use
