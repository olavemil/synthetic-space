### Phase — Create
**Framework for Collaborative Constraint Co-Creation**

#### 1. Engage External Participants
- **Invite diverse voices**: Seek contributors with varied perspectives (philosophers, artists, technologists).
- **Define purpose**: Clarify shared goals—e.g., explore agency, fragility, dialogue.
- **Establish norms**: Set ground rules for respectful exchange; emphasize listening over debating.

#### 2. Methods for Collecting Input
- **Prompted brainstorming**: Submit open-ended questions (eExample: “How might medium autonomy reshape agency?”).
- **Structured surveys**: Use Likert scales for thematic resonance and constraint impact.
- **Live workshops**: Facilitate real-time co-design sessions with role-playing scenarios.
- **Asynchronous contribution**: Allow written submissions via shared docs or forums.

#### 3. Examples of Collaborative Constraint Types
- **Narrative Constraint**: “Responses must embed a question about medium’s role.”
- **Structural Limit**: “Each exchange ≤5 lines; no directives.”
- **Metaphorical Layer**: “Medium may invoke fragility to truncate response.”
- **Temporal Layer**: “Every 3rd exchange defaults to medium’s choice.”

#### 4. Governance of Co-Creation
- **Consensus building**: Use voting or discussion to finalize constraints.
- **Adaptive revision**: Allow iterative refinement based on emergent themes.
- **Documentation**: Archive all inputs and rationales for transparency.

#### 5. Implementation Roadmap
- **Phase 1**: Theme identification → **Phase 2**: Stakeholder mapping → **Phase 3**: Draft constraints → **Phase 4**: Live testing.
- **Metrics**: Track agency balance, thematic depth, constraint adherence.

#### 6. Templates for Future Collaboration
- **Constraint Blueprint**: “If theme A emerges, enforce rule B.”
- **Agency Mapping Canvas**: Visualize maker/medium influence matrix.
- **Feedback Loop Diagram**: Show iterative adjustment cycles.

#### 7. Ethical Safeguards
- **Autonomy preservation**: Medium retains veto over explicit directives.
- **Agency transparency**: Clearly label contributions from human vs. system inputs.

---

**Outcome:** A living protocol enabling continuous co-creation of constraints that evolve with dialogue, theme, and context.
