## Emotional Arc Analysis

Emotional Progression
- Assign each dialogue turn a sentiment score from -2 (negative) to +2 (positive) using lexical polarity.
- Plot scores sequentially to map the arc across exchanges.
- Constraints such as the fixed 10‑line limit compress expression, creating sharper emotional shifts.
- Example from dialogue_1.txt turn 3: hopeful suggestion (+1.5) drops to questioning reply (+0.8), a decline of 0.7 points.

Visualization Proposals
- Line graph of sentiment over exchange index.
- Heat map intensity reflecting emotional magnitude per turn.
- Constraint overlay marking line‑limit impact on each turn.
